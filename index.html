What is Revealr?

Revealr is a platform designed for consensual information exchange, especially for sensitive information such as school admission results.

Start by entering your information and sharing preferences, then invite someone else to do the same. The system cross-references both sets of preferences, ensuring that information is revealed only when both parties have mutually consented to the exchange.
